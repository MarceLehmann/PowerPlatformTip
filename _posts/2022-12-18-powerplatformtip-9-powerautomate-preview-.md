## 💡 Challenge
You've created a complex flow in Power Automate and want to make sure everything works as expected—without activating it and taking risks?

## ✅ Solution
Use the built-in **Test** feature in Power Automate to try your flow in advance. This way you can identify errors and optimization opportunities before the flow goes live.

## 🔧 How It's Done

**1. Open your flow in Power Automate**

🔸 Go to the flow's edit view.

**2. Select Test (top-right corner)**

🔸 Choose **Manually** and provide the trigger inputs.

**3. Run the test and follow each step**

🔸 Watch the execution and inspect the inputs/outputs of every action.

**4. Analyze the results**

🔸 Adjust the flow if necessary and re-run the test.

## 🎉 Result
The Test feature saves you time, prevents errors in live operation, and lets you improve flows iteratively.

## 🌟 Key Advantages

🔸 Detect and fix errors early.

🔸 No risk to the production system.

🔸 More efficient development and testing.

## 🛠️ FAQ

**1. Is the Test feature available for all flows?**

It's available for most flow types, especially automated and scheduled flows.

**2. Is real data processed during a test?**

Usually test data is used, but you can also simulate your own inputs manually.

**3. Can I run multiple test runs?**

Yes, you can run as many tests as you like until your flow works optimally.
